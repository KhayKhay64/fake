ABORTION

An abortion is a procedure to end a pregnancy. An abortion is the early termination of a pregnancy, which can happen spontaneously, as in the case of a miscarriage, or can happen when the pregnancy is ended by medical or surgical means. It is sometimes called a "Medical abortion" or "abortion with pills." "Procedural abortion", a procedure to remove the pregnancy from the uterus. An unsafe abortion is the termination of a pregnancy by people lacking the necessary skills, or in an environment lacking minimal medical standards, or both. An unsafe abortion is a life-threatening procedure.
