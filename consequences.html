Abortion causes a weakening of the cervix, which increases a woman’s risk of future pre-term deliveries. After two or more abortions a woman’s risk of preterm birth increases by between 51% and 62%.
Studies show that abortion increases a woman’s risk of breast cancer. A 2013 analysis revealed a 44% increased risk of breast cancer among females who had at least one induced abortion.Women who had undergone an abortion experienced an increased risk of mental health problems.
It can also lead to problems such as infection and damage to the uterus and can lead to infertility and increased risks of future childbearing.

So, about infection. A mother rarely gets the infection after an abortion. Only around 1 in 100 get an infection after an abortion. Perforation of the uterus is an uncommon but potentially serious complication that happens after abortion. There may also be damage to the genital tract and internal organs because of the insertion of dangerous objects into the vagina or the anus.
